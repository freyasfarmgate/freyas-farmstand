Manage Today's Produce

Your produce list is managed directly in your Google Sheet. Use the button below to open it — changes show on the website within a minute.

📊 Open Produce Sheet

How to update your produce list:

  1. Click Open Produce Sheet above
  2. Find the row for the item you want to change
  3. In the Status column, type one of: Available, Limited, or Unavailable
  4. To add new produce, add a new row with Name, Description, Emoji, and Status
  5. Save the sheet — your website updates automatically!
💡 Tip: Bookmark the Google Sheet on your phone — then every morning you can quickly update what's available before customers arrive!

Manage Recipes

Recipes are stored in your Google Sheet on the Recipes tab. Open it below to add or edit recipes.

📊 Open Recipes Sheet

How to add a recipe:

  1. Click Open Recipes Sheet and go to the Recipes tab
  2. Add a new row at the bottom
  3. Fill in: Title, Tag (e.g. "🍓 Strawberries"), Description, Time, Serves
  4. For ingredients, separate each one with a pipe | character
    Example: 500g strawberries|1 cup sugar|8 basil leaves
  5. For steps, also separate with a pipe |
    Example: Hull strawberries|Combine with sugar|Simmer 25 min
  6. The recipe appears on the website straight away!
